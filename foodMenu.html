Weekly food menu

Every day: Brazilian style cooked beans.
Monday: Kung Pao chicken, rice, farofa and onions on butter.

Wednesday: Rice with pork ribs and black beans.
Thursday: Pasta with bolognese sauce or pasta with white sauce and mushrooms.
Friday: Roasted chicken, rice, brown beans and mashed potatoes.
